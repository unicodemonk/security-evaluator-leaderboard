## üé§ Audio Recording Tips

### Voice Settings
- Volume: Speak at 70-80% of normal volume (prevents clipping)
- Pace: Slightly slower than conversation
- Clarity: Enunciate technical terms
- Energy: Maintain enthusiasm throughout

### Record Separately
1. Record video first (with temp audio or silent)
2. Record voiceover while watching video
3. Sync in editing software
4. Easier to fix mistakes this way

## üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

### "Leaderboard not loading"
- Refresh page
- Check internet connection
- Clear browser cache
- Use incognito mode

### "Can't see cursor in recording"
- Enable "Show mouse clicks" in settings
- Use a larger cursor
- Add yellow circle in post-production

### "Audio has echo"
- Record in smaller room
- Use headphones to monitor
- Remove hard surfaces nearby
- Add blankets for sound dampening

### "Screen looks blurry"
- Increase recording resolution
- Use native screen resolution
- Disable browser zoom
- Clean monitor/screen
